== Description ==

<p>Introducing Scroll Header Pro, tool for easy creating one-page sites and astonishing headings.<br />
	This section allows you to use neat titles in front of any Pagelines section on the page. It fully allows you to take control over Scrol Spy. That way your page becomes perfect place to load as many sections as you wish, providing an easy to use manner. Both Lettering and FitText engines are included so you can take it as an advantage if titles are crucial part of your page design.
<h3>Features</h3>
<p>
	<strong>Add Title, Heading and Subheading text.</strong><br />
	<strong>Scroll Spy ready.</strong><br />
	Works automaticaly with Scroll Spy. If you whant to use section clone as non-navigation heading on pages with ScrollSpy, you can disable Scroll Spy usage.<br />
	<strong>Customize Look and feel</strong><br />
	Choose Heading and subheading font.<br />
	Choose Heading HTML tag (from H1 -  H6).<br />
	Use Custom Heading Class. Default is .page-header.<br />
	Use FitText for creating fluid Headings.<br />
	Use Lettering for full control over every letter, word or line of your heading.<br />
	<strong>Scroll Spy shortcode</strong><br />
	Easy to use shortcode for inserting navigation heading in content.
</p>
<h3>Setup Instructions</h3>
<ol>
	<li>Create a page.</li>
	<li>Drag &amp; Drop Scroll Spy on page.</li>
	<li>Drag &amp; Drop Scroll Header Pro on page template and/or content area. Create as many clones as you need.</li>
	<li>Add Title (required for use with Scroll Spy), heading and subheading texts.</li>
	<li>Customize your headings.</li>
	<li>Drag &amp; Drop other sections between Headings.</li>
	<li>Voila. Scrollable one-page site is ready to go.</li>
</ol>
