== Description ==

<p>
<h3>SHP NOW WITH PARALLAX<small>  ~ Create a one-page site in a breeze ~</small></h3>
Perfect for adding scroll ready headings between sections. Now it comes with easy to use parallax effect. Fits on any screen. Create one page site in minutes.
</p>

<p>
<h3>Features</h3>
<br>
<ul>
	<li><strong style="font-size: 1.1em;">Scroll - ready</strong> - works automatically with <a href="http://www.pagelines.com/store/sections/scroll-nav/" target="_blank">Scroll Nav</a> and Scroll Spy.</li>
	<li><strong style="font-size: 1.1em;">Built in Parallax Effect</strong> - for effective one-page sites.</li>
	<li><strong style="font-size: 1.1em;">Named Metatabs</strong> - title text from section options is used as clone's metatab name.</li>
	<li><strong style="font-size: 1.1em;">Customize Look &amp; Feel</strong> - Choose heading <strong>font, HTML tag </strong> and <strong> custom class</strong>.</li>
	<li><strong style="font-size: 1.1em;">Big and Responsive</strong> - heading text that fits to any screen.</li>
	<li><strong style="font-size: 1.1em;">Use Lettering.js</strong> - for advanced control over every letter, word or line of one heading.</li>
	<li><strong style="font-size: 1.1em;">Scroll Spy shortcode</strong> - easy to use shortcode for inserting ScrollSpy-ready headings in content.</li>
</ul>
</p>

<p>
<h3>Setup Instructions</h3>
<br>
<h4>One-page site</h4>
<ol>
	<li>Go to Pagelines "Drag &amp; Drop".</li>
	<li>Drag &amp; Drop Scroll Spy or use <a href="http://www.pagelines.com/store/sections/scroll-nav/" target="_blank">Scroll Nav</a>.</li>
	<li>Drag &amp; Drop Pagelines sections that you need.</li>
	<li>Drag &amp; Drop Scroll Header Pro.</li>
	<li>Create as many Scroll Header Pro clones as you need.</li>
	<li>Place your clones between sections and go to edit your page.</li>
	<li>Add title, heading and subheading text to Scroll Header Pro section clones.</li>
	<li>Customize your headings with options.</li>
	<li>Voila. Scrollable one-page site is ready to go.</li>
</ol>
<br>
<h4>Splash text</h4>
<ol>
	<li>Go to Pagelines "Drag &amp; Drop" menu.</li>
	<li>Drag &amp; Drop Scroll Header Pro.</li>
	<li>Go to page metapanel.</li>
	<li>Customize your heading with options.</li>
	<li>Apply custom style to your section (use example mixins from demo page or add your own custom style).</li>
</ol>
</p>
